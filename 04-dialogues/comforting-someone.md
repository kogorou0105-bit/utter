# Comforting Someone

## Dialogue 1: Almost A Car Accident

A: I almost got into a car accident yesterday.

B: Oh my god, that sounds terrifying. Are you okay?

A: Yeah, I am fine, but I was really scared.

B: I can imagine. That must have really shaken you up. I am just glad you are safe.

A: Thanks. I still keep thinking about it.

B: That makes sense. Do you want to talk about what happened?

## Dialogue 2: Rejected By First-Choice University

A: I did not get into my first-choice university.

B: I am really sorry to hear that. I know how much that meant to you.

A: I feel like I failed.

B: I get why it feels that way, but this does not define you. You worked really hard, and I am proud of you for trying your best.

A: I just do not know what to do now.

B: We do not have to figure everything out right now. Do you want comfort, advice, or just someone to listen?
