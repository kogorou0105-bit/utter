# Daily Speaking Prompts

Use one prompt per day. Speak out loud for 2 to 5 minutes.

## Emotional Support

1. Someone tells you they almost had an accident. Comfort them.
2. Someone tells you they failed an important exam. Comfort them.
3. Someone tells you they did not get the offer they wanted. Comfort them.

## Conversation Continuation

1. Someone says they had a terrible day. Ask 3 follow-up questions.
2. Someone says they are nervous about tomorrow. Respond naturally.
3. Someone says they feel lost. Show empathy and ask what they need.

## Self-Expression

1. Talk about a time when you felt disappointed.
2. Talk about a time when you were scared but safe in the end.
3. Talk about a goal that matters to you.
