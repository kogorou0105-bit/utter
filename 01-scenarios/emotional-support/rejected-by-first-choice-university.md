# Rejected By First-Choice University

## Situation

A girl told me her first-choice university did not accept her, and she feels really sad.

## What I Wanted To Say In Chinese

听到这个我很遗憾。我知道这对你很重要，你难过是很正常的。你已经很努力了，这不代表你不够好。

## Natural English

- I am really sorry to hear that.
- I know how much that meant to you.
- It is completely okay to feel disappointed.
- You worked really hard, and this does not define you.
- I am proud of you for trying your best.

## Warmer Version

- I wish I could make this hurt less for you.
- I know this really hurts right now, but I still believe good things are ahead for you.
- You are still the same smart and capable person, even if this result was not what you hoped for.

## Follow-Up Questions

- Do you want to talk about it?
- What are you thinking of doing next?
- Is there anything I can do to help?
- Do you want comfort, advice, or just someone to listen?

## Useful Patterns

- I know how much + noun + meant to you.
- It is okay to feel + emotion.
- This does not define you.
- I am proud of you for + gerund.

## Less Natural Or Less Helpful

- Do not be sad.
- You can apply again.
- Maybe that university is not good.
- It is not a big deal.

## Speaking Drill

Say a response in 3 steps:

1. Acknowledge the disappointment.
2. Validate her feelings.
3. Remind her that the result does not define her.
