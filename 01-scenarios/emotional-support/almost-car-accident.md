# Almost Got Into A Car Accident

## Situation

A girl told me she almost got into a car accident yesterday.

## What I Wanted To Say In Chinese

天啊，听起来太吓人了。你现在还好吗？幸好你没事。你一定被吓坏了吧。

## Natural English

- Oh my god, that sounds terrifying. Are you okay?
- I am really glad you are safe.
- That must have really shaken you up.
- I am so sorry that happened. That must have been scary.
- Do you want to talk about what happened?

## Warmer Version

- I am just really glad you are okay.
- I wish I could give you a hug right now.
- Take your time. That sounds like a lot to process.

## Follow-Up Questions

- Were you alone when it happened?
- Did you get hurt at all?
- How are you feeling now?
- Has it been on your mind since then?

## Useful Patterns

- That sounds + adjective.
- I am really glad + good result.
- That must have + past participle.
- Do you want to talk about it?

## Less Natural Or Less Helpful

- It is very dangerous.
- You should be careful.
- That is unlucky.

## Speaking Drill

Say a response in 3 steps:

1. Show shock or concern.
2. Ask if she is okay.
3. Express relief and care.
