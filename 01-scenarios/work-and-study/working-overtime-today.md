# Working Overtime Today

## Situation

Someone asked me if I was too busy today. I was working overtime because I had unfinished work.

## What I Wanted To Say In Chinese

是的，今天有点忙。我在加班，因为有些工作白天没做完。

因为上周的团队团建，我有点落后了，导致任务没有按时交付，质量也没有达到我想要的标准。所以我现在需要补上。

## Natural English

- Yeah, I am a bit busy today. I am working overtime because I did not finish some work earlier.
- Yeah, today has been pretty busy. I still have some work to finish, so I am working late.
- Kind of. I have been catching up on some unfinished work.
- A little bit. I am working late tonight because I still have a few things to wrap up.
- I have to be responsible for my work. If something is not finished, I need to stay late and get it done.
- I just feel like I should take responsibility for my work. If I do not finish it during the day, I have to work overtime.
- I fell a bit behind because of last week's team-building event, so I did not deliver the task on time or to the standard I wanted.
- Last week's team-building event took up some time, and I ended up falling behind on the task. I need to catch up and make sure it is done properly.
- Because of the team-building event last week, I did not finish the task on time or at the quality I expected, so I am working late to fix that.

## More Casual Version

- Yeah, a bit. I am still working on a few things.
- Kind of. I have got some unfinished work to take care of.
- Yeah, I am working late today. Nothing too serious, just some stuff I need to finish.
- I just want to make sure I finish what I am responsible for.
- If I do not get it done during the day, I usually stay late and finish it.
- I fell behind a bit last week because of a team event, so now I am catching up.
- I did not finish it as well as I wanted, so I am trying to make it right.

## Warmer Version

- Yeah, a little busy today, but I am glad you asked.
- I am working late tonight, but I can still chat for a bit.
- I have some work to finish, but talking to you is a nice break.

## If I Want To Keep The Conversation Going

- How about you? Was your day busy too?
- I should be done later tonight. How was your day?
- I am almost done with work. What are you up to?

## Useful Patterns

- I am working overtime today.
- I am working late tonight.
- I still have some work to finish.
- I have a few things to wrap up.
- I am catching up on unfinished work.
- I have to be responsible for my work.
- I should take responsibility for my work.
- I want to make sure I finish what I am responsible for.
- I need to stay late and get it done.
- I fell behind because of + reason.
- I did not deliver it on time.
- It was not up to the standard I wanted.
- I need to catch up.
- I need to make it right.

## Less Natural Or Incorrect

- I am overwork today.
- I did not finish it in workday.
- Because of some work I did not finish in workday.

## Notes

- `overwork` is usually a verb, not an adjective in this sentence.
- Say `I am working overtime` or `I am working late`.
- `workday` usually means a working day in general. Here, `earlier today`, `during the day`, or `at work` sounds more natural.
