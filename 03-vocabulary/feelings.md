# Feelings

## sad / upset / disappointed / devastated

### sad

General word for feeling unhappy.

- She looked sad after hearing the news.

### upset

Emotionally affected. It can mean sad, angry, worried, or disturbed.

- She was upset about what happened.

### disappointed

Unhappy because something did not meet expectations.

- She was disappointed that she did not get into her first-choice university.

### devastated

Extremely shocked, hurt, or sad.

- He was devastated after losing someone close to him.

## shaken up

Emotionally disturbed after a scary or shocking experience.

- That accident really shook her up.
- You must be pretty shaken up after that.
