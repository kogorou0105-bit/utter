# Asking Follow-Up Questions

Use follow-up questions to keep a conversation natural and show real interest.

## General

- What happened?
- How did that happen?
- How did you feel when that happened?
- What did you do after that?
- What are you thinking of doing next?

## Gentle

- Do you want to talk about it?
- Can I ask what happened?
- Has it been on your mind?
- Would it help to talk it through?

## When Someone Is Upset

- Do you want comfort, advice, or just someone to listen?
- Do you want me to stay with you for a bit?
- Is there anything I can do right now?
