# Empathy

Use these expressions when someone shares something difficult, stressful, or emotional.

## Showing Concern

- Are you okay?
- Are you doing okay now?
- How are you feeling now?
- That sounds really scary.
- That must have been really hard.

## Validating Feelings

- It makes sense that you feel that way.
- It is completely okay to feel disappointed.
- I would probably feel the same way.
- I know how much that meant to you.

## Expressing Relief

- I am really glad you are safe.
- I am just glad you are okay.
- Thank god nothing worse happened.

## Offering Support

- Do you want to talk about it?
- I am here if you want to talk.
- Is there anything I can do to help?
- Do you want comfort, advice, or just someone to listen?

## Avoid

- Do not be sad.
- It is not a big deal.
- You should be more careful.
- Everything happens for a reason.
